Refund Eligibility

Eligible for Refund

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • You request a refund within 14 days of purchase
  • Your account is in good standing (no policy violations)
  • You have not exceeded fair usage limits (typically not applicable during trial period)

Not Eligible for Refund

You are not eligible for a refund if:

  • 14 days have passed since your purchase
  • Your account has been terminated due to policy violations
  • You have previously received a refund for the same subscription period
  • You have used the Service extensively beyond normal trial usage
  • You are requesting a refund for fraud, misrepresentation, or abuse
